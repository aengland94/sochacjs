Application of ODEs Videos

Sample Solar System Video

A sample demo of the Solar System simulation. The movement of the bodies are calculated live using a gravitational force, historic data as a starting point, and ODEs for precision.

FDL Video

A demo of the Force Directed Layout (FDL) simulation. The simulation shows the conections of simple and complex networks. The movement of the nodes are calculated live using a gravitational force, a spring force, and ODEs for precision.

Solar System and Jupiter Moons Video

A complete demo of the Solar System and Jupiter Moons simulation. The movement of the bodies are calculated live using a gravitational force, historic data as a starting point, and ODEs for precision.

Neural Network Video

A demo of the Neural Network simulation. Like the FDL, this simulation shows the connections between nodes in simple and complex networks. Instead of moving the nodes, they are charged with varying amount of "voltage" that causes them to light up. The higher the voltage, the more white the node appears. It uses the idea that "neurons that fire together wire together" to show the connections. The precision of the calculations were made by using ODEs.
